## Optional: Configure your stream to send logs to EU accounts [#configure-eu-stream]

If you have a New Relic account in our EU data center:

1. Follow standard procedures to [create your delivery stream](#create-delivery-stream).

2. Once your stream has been created, navigate to the [Amazon Kinesis Console](https://console.aws.amazon.com/kinesis/), and click <DoNotTranslate>**Delivery streams**</DoNotTranslate>.

3. Click the name of your newly created delivery stream, and select the <DoNotTranslate>**Configuration**</DoNotTranslate> tab.

4. Next to the <DoNotTranslate>**Destination settings**</DoNotTranslate> section, click <DoNotTranslate>**Edit**</DoNotTranslate>.

5. Update the value for <DoNotTranslate>**HTTP endpoint URL**</DoNotTranslate> to our Firehose endpoint for EU accounts:

    ```
    https://aws-api.eu.newrelic.com/firehose/v1
    ```

6. Click <DoNotTranslate>**Save changes**</DoNotTranslate> to ensure your stream is updated.
