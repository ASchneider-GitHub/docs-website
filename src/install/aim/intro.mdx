AI monitoring lets you track the performance of the different apps, services, and databases that make up your AI assistant. When your AI receives a prompt, AI monitoring captures the process your AI used to generate a response, tracking the response from external LLMs, vector stores, and more. 

Setting up involves installing one of our language agents and enabling AI monitoring at the config level. When you're done, the agent will recognize data at the AI layer of your app, then store it in our databases.
