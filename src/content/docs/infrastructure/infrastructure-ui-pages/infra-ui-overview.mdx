        ## Overview [#overview] 

        When you open the [infrastructure UI](https://one.newrelic.com/infra/infrastructure/hosts), you start out on the [<DNT>**Hosts**</DNT> page](/docs/infrastructure/infrastructure-ui-pages/infra-hosts-ui-page), which gives you an overview of the state of your system, and gives you many options for exploring your hosts in more detail. 

        Other pages in the <DNT>**Overview**</DNT> section include: 

        * <DNT>**Kubernetes**</DNT>: This data is reported by our [Kubernetes integration](/docs/kubernetes-pixie/kubernetes-integration/get-started/introduction-kubernetes-integration), if enabled. 
        * <DNT>**Network**</DNT>: This takes you to our [network performance monitoring UI](/docs/network-performance-monitoring/get-started/npm-introduction). 
        * <DNT>**Inventory**</DNT>: Here you can check your infrastructure configuration for upgrades and configuration drift. [Learn more about the inventory UI](/docs/infrastructure/infrastructure-ui-pages/infrastructure-inventory-page-search-your-entire-infrastructure).
        * <DNT>**Events**</DNT>: A timeline of important system activity in your infrastructure stack. [Learn more about the events UI](/docs/infrastructure/infrastructure-ui-pages/infrastructure-events-page-live-feed-every-config-change).
    </Side>

        ## Integrations [#integrations]

        The <DNT>**Integration**</DNT> section of the UI includes telemetry from any infrastructure integrations you've set up. These integrations can include: 

        * Cloud service integrations, including [AWS](/docs/infrastructure/amazon-integrations/get-started/introduction-aws-integrations), [Azure](/docs/infrastructure/microsoft-azure-integrations/get-started/introduction-azure-monitoring-integrations), and [Google Cloud Platform](/docs/infrastructure/google-cloud-platform-integrations/get-started/introduction-google-cloud-platform-integrations) 
        * Third-party services: our many infrastructure-related integrations, including Apache, Cassandra, StatsD, RabbitMQ, and many more. To browse and add these, go to the [<DNT>**Add data**</DNT> page](https://newrelic.com/instant-observability?category=infrastructure-and-os) in the platform. 
    </Side>
</SideBySide>
