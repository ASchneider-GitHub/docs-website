You can set limits with certain event attributes in your APM agent. Keep in mind that adjusting limits:

    * Tells your agent how much to sample events or spans. 
    * May mean less sampling of your d ata, which gives a complete picture of your app's performance, but also requires more memory usage and has ingest cost implications.
